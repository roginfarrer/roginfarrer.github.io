Oh, hey.

I’m Rogin Farrer and I’m a theatrical designer and pixel-pusher based in the Greater Boston area. I was born and raised just west of Boston.

I drink my coffee black, constantly listen to podcasts and new music, and believe great design incorporates all elements of the production working together as a unified whole. I’m a big fan of art, literature, and philosophy, and appreciate the ways they intertwine together and with performance.

This spring I will graduate from Vassar College with a B.A. in Drama, and afterwards I will begin working for the Gloucester Stage Company as their Scenic Design/Carpentry Apprentice.

Between my studies and production work, I design and develop websites. I’ve worked with HTML, CSS, and Wordpress for years, and am becoming increasingly fluent in Javascript, PHP, Jekyll, and mobile responsive practices. I have a soft spot for typography.
